A Love Story

Our Story

Some love stories are written in the stars — ours was written in laughter, adventure, and the quiet moments in between. Here's the journey that brought us to this moment, and to you.

The Journey

Milestones

  1. 2021

    How We Met

    It was one of those ordinary days that turned out to be anything but ordinary. A mutual friend introduced us at a gathering, and from the very first conversation we knew there was something special between us. The hours flew by and neither of us wanted the evening to end.

  2. Early 2022

    Our First Date

    Nervous, excited, and full of hope — our first official date was an evening we both still talk about. Over dinner and long walks, we discovered just how much we had in common, and laughed until our cheeks hurt. By the end of the night, we both knew this was only the beginning.

  3. 2022–2023

    Adventures Together

    From weekend road trips to spontaneous travels abroad, we spent the next years exploring the world — and each other. Every new place added a new chapter to our story: sunsets on cliff edges, dancing in the rain, quiet mornings with coffee and no plans. Life together had become our greatest adventure.

  4. 2024

    The Proposal

    Under a sky full of stars, in a place that had always felt like ours, the question was finally asked. Through happy tears and a resounding “yes,” we began the next chapter of our story — planning the celebration of a lifetime, surrounded by the people who matter most.

  5. August 29, 2027

    The Big Day

    And now — the day we've been dreaming of. Surrounded by family and friends in the beautiful mountains of Canmore, Alberta, we'll stand together and promise each other forever. We cannot wait to celebrate with each and every one of you.
