The dress code is Garden Party Chic. Think elevated smart casual — floral dresses, midi or maxi gowns, linen suits, or a polished blazer-and-trousers combination. We are celebrating in the beautiful mountains of Canmore, so heels on the ceremony lawn may not be ideal; block heels, wedges, or dressy flats are a great choice.

Parking is available at Stewart Creek Golf & Country Club. Please park on the far end of the lot to avoid overcrowding near the ceremony tent. The cocktail hour and reception are at Bridgette Bar Canmore (1030 Spring Creek Dr) — a short drive or rideshare from the ceremony venue.

We recommend ridesharing between the ceremony (Stewart Creek) and the reception (Bridgette Bar Canmore). The two venues are a short drive apart in Canmore. If you are staying at The Malcolm Hotel, Bridgette Bar is very close by.

Our wedding is an adults-only evening. We hope you will enjoy a night off and celebrate with us! If you have specific questions, please reach out at majaandjack@gmail.com.

The ceremony begins at 4:00 PM and will last approximately 30–45 minutes, followed by cocktail hour starting at 5:30 PM at Bridgette Bar. We recommend arriving by 3:45 PM so you can find your seat before we begin.
